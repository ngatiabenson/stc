Admission Requirements

Welcome to the official admissions gateway for Spiritans Technical College. To maintain our high academic standard and fulfill national educational criteria in Kenya. Spiritans Technical College (STC) is a catholic sponsored college, which fodter Christian values, guided by our school motto PARATUS AD VITAM(Prepared for life). It is regisrered under the ministry of education(TVETA) to offer different technical courses. This offer is based on your qualifications and is subject to verification by the college on the date of admission. Applicants must meet the structured benchmarks below.

Academic Qualifications

  • Diploma Courses (KNEC/CDACC): KCSE Mean Grade C- (Minus) and above or equivalent standard.
  • Certificate Programs: KCSE Mean Grade D (Plain) and above or equivalent standard.
  • Artisan & Short Courses (NITA): Open to all passionate candidates seeking practical skill sets (KCSE/KCPE certificate is an added advantage).

Mandatory On-Boarding Documents

Please bring original copies and photocopies of the following items on reporting day:

  • National ID Card or Valid Birth Certificate
  • KCSE Result Slip / Highschool Leaving Certificate
  • KCPE Certificate / result slip
  • Two recent, passport-sized color photographs
