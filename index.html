Who We Are

Rooted in Purpose

Spiritans Technical College was founded by the Holy Ghost Fathers (Spiritans) to bring world-class vocational training to the heart of Makueni County.

Our Vision

Empowered by the Holy Spirit to discover, innovate, enhance, care and inspire.

Our Mission

To be a caring center of excellence enriched by gospel values to genuinely impact the society and the environment.

Our Philosophy

We develop wholesome, well-rounded persons that easily fit into civil, business and management communities with effective, focused and visionary contributions.

"Spiritans College offers education and training aimed at reducing poverty through provision of employment skills, competences, self-reliance and improvement of human life."
