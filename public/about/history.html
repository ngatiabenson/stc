About STC

Spiritans Technical College(STC)is a catholic sponsored college, which foster Christian Values, guided by our school motto, PARATUS AD VITUM (prepared for life). It is registered under the ministry of education (TVETA) to offer different technical courses.

Established under robust educational frameworks, Spiritans Technical College was founded to close the critical technical skills gap in the region. Driven by gospel values and a profound commitment to human development, we provide industry-validated technical competence frameworks designed to empower youth with self-reliant, market-ready capabilities.

Our training matrices combine strict academic rigor with extensive hands-on experimentation. This ensures that every trainee moving through our academic paths leaves ready to make an immediate, tangible impact on industry operations, technical innovations, and structural community development within Kenya's evolving economic landscape.

Certified National Educational Parameters

All academic programs at STC are fully monitored and vetted under certified quality metrics governed by KNEC, CDACC, and NITA testing registries within Kenya.

Our Mission

To be a caring center of technical excellence, enriched by gospel values, dedicated to equipping learners with practical skills and genuinely impacting society.

Our Vision

Empowered by the Holy Spirit to discover talents, renovate skills, enhance innovation, and inspire absolute excellence in technical education.
