Celebrating Diversity: STC Cultural Day

At Spiritans Technical College, technical innovation blends seamlessly with cultural preservation. Our annual Cultural Day serves as a critical milestone event where students showcase Kenya's diverse heritage through traditional music, food curation, and creative fashion runway systems organized by our design departments.

This integration fosters soft skills like team leadership, cross-cultural collaboration, and community building—qualities that are highly valued by corporate recruiters looking for well-rounded graduates.
